About

Builds things.

Jason Cerf builds AI agents that do real work for real people. His current focus is Agentify — a platform that gives individuals and businesses a dedicated AI agent via SMS, handling customer service, scheduling, outreach, and operations around the clock.

He also runs Driftwood Digital, helping businesses deploy AI systems that actually change how their operations run — not demos, but working tools. His background spans music, technology, and the trades: he owns a catalog of 200,000+ recordings and has built businesses from the ground up across several industries.

On weekends — and occasionally on weekdays when the deadline is loose — he goes rock climbing. Gravity remains undefeated but the scoreline is contested.
